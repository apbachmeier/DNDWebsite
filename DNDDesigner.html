This website has been designed as a resource for all levels of Dungeons and Dragons players. Whether you are new to the game, or have been playing for years, we hope that you are able to learn something in order to make your next session more enjoyable.

Have you ever looked at the 316 pages in the players handbook and thought to yourself "fuck that"? We have created a concise guide to help you navigate some of the most important information in the player's handbook.
